# CoVound: Product & Vision
**Truth and Trust Founder Companion**

## 1. The Core Concept
**CoVound** is an Active AI Security Firewall designed to intercept, neutralize, and educate users about sophisticated social engineering attacks, specifically SEO poisoning and AI search hallucinations.

### The Mission
> **"Turn Your Trauma Into Their Takedown."**
> **"Ubah Kepanikan Menjadi Keadilan."**

### The Marketplace of Truth
We are building a crowdsourced \"Source of Truth\" for financial contacts. By crowdsourcing incident data first, we create a reliable dataset that powers a real-time redaction shield.

---

## 2. Competitive Landscape & USP
1.  **Zero-Trust Architecture:** Unlike blocklists, CoVound only permits mathematically verified contact data to pass through.
2.  **Omnichannel Interception:** Protects users across traditional Web Search (Extension) and next-gen AI assistants (MCP).
3.  **Digital ER Triage UX:** Transforms a security block into a calming, educational experience based on the **Dr. Gia persona**.
4.  **Legal Empowerment:** Provides reporters with **Verified Digital Forensic Records** formatted for Indonesian Police (STPL) submission.

---

## 3. Risk Management
- **False Positives:** Managed via SLA-driven registry updates and community \"Report as Safe\" features.
- **Registry Poisoning:** Prevented via **Domain-Anchored PKI**; institutions must sign updates using keys tied to their root domain.
- **Privacy (UU PDP):** Sanitization engine redacts PII from evidence using AI Surgery; sensitive stories move to cold storage after 24 hours.
